Introduction to QuickSwap Exchange
QuickSwap Exchange is a decentralized exchange (DEX) built on the Polygon network, formerly known as Matic. Launched in February 2021, QuickSwap has rapidly gained traction within the DeFi community, offering users a seamless and lightning-fast trading experience. As an automated market maker (AMM) DEX, QuickSwap enables users to swap various ERC-20 tokens with minimal slippage and low transaction fees, making it an attractive alternative to traditional centralized exchanges.

Automated Market Making (AMM) Model
QuickSwap operates on an automated market making (AMM) model, wherein liquidity is provided by liquidity providers (LPs) rather than order books. Users can trade tokens directly against liquidity pools, which are filled with various ERC-20 tokens and governed by smart contracts. This AMM model ensures continuous liquidity and eliminates the need for order matching, resulting in faster transactions and lower fees.
